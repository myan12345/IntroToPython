About This Course

This Intro to Python Course was a project I worked on during the Summer of 2021 to improve my teaching skills and demonstrate what I have learned in Python. Most students don't pick up programming until a much later age, so making this accessible to people my age was a goal of mine. I hope that this course is a great learning experience and helps form more bright minds in coding at my age.
